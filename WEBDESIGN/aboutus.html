ABOUT US

TURASSIC is the only rent a car service provider that provides a distinctive transportation solution to the travelers during their stay at any hotel within our partner hotels network, along with many other services and programs that meant to serve and facilitate our members trip. Our services aimed to provide the hotel guests with the privilege of driving a car whenever they need and at any time during their hotel stay. Wither the car reservation has been made in-advance or on-spot, our promise is to present a hassles free, simple and flexible car rental experience. Hotel Guests should skip all the long car rental process (Pickup and Return) at the airport, instead they will get their rented car from the hotel, then return it back to the same spot.

VISION

To redefine the car rental concept and lifting it to a new level were hassles should become a history, our hotel’s guest members should enjoy a totally Hassle Free experience, totally new feeling of comfort and peace of mind when it comes to car rental arrangement, leaving the Conventional Car-Rental methods behind.

MISSION
