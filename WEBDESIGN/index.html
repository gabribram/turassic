HOW IT WORKS

Easy steps to your next adventure!

At TURASSIC we did our best to make the car rental a real exciting and interesting experience. Latest technologies and hi-tech solutions were utilized to the privilege of our members.

HOW IT WORKS

1

Register

Sign up to our website or mobile app and complete the registration requirements to get your membership ID. You need this ID to process any car reservation

2

Reserve

For instant reservation, enter your membership ID at the hotel self-service kiosk, select your car and set your expected rental period, take out your car key from the built-in key compartment.

3

Drive

The display screen on the kiosk will direct you to the car location, upon reaching your car make a quick check up, get in the driver’s seat and enjoy your trip.

4

Return

Return the car to its original spot, move towered the self-service kiosk and scan the car key QR code, drop off the key at the built-in key compartment to end your rental period.
